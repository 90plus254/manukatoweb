Our Story

Manukato Brands was conceived in the year 2016, with a simple desire to bring exquisite, affordable fragrances to everyone. Inspired by the rich scents of natural combinations that would leave a mark on memory raather than physcial touch, we embarked on opening our shop in 2017 at the second floor of our current location, where we would share space with another business(shout out to Tony Karembo) of Karembo Beauty spa, ofcourse, just like many other young businesses we encountered a myriad of challenges, but our dream and passion for fragrance kept us going.

Who We Serve

We serve all individuals who appreciate quality fragrances and seek unique scents that reflect their personal style. Whether you're looking for a signature scent for everyday wear or a special fragrance for a memorable occasion, Manukato Brands offers a diverse range of perfumes to suit every taste and budget.
